How to Reconstitute

Simple version: keep everything clean, add the water slowly, and do not shake.

1
Let the vial warm upRemove from cold storage and let it reach room temperature before opening.
2
Sanitize the topUse an alcohol wipe to clean the rubber stopper before adding anything.
3
Add BAC water slowlyUse a sterile syringe and let the water run down the side of the vial.
4
Gently swirlSwirl gently until dissolved. Do not shake the vial.
